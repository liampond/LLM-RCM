The relative minor of B major is **G# minor**. The melodic minor scale has different ascending and descending forms.

### Ascending G# melodic minor scale:
G# - A# - B - C# - D# - E# - F## - G#

### Descending G# melodic minor scale:
G# - F# - E - D# - C# - B - A# - G#

- The **ascending form** raises the 6th and 7th scale degrees (E# and F##).
- The **descending form** typically follows the natural minor scale.

In the treble clef, use the key signature of G# minor (five sharps: F#, C#, G#, D#, A#) and add accidentals (E# and F##) as needed for the ascending form.
