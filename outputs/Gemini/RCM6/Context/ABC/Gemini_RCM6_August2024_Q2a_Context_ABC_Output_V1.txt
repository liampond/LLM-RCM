1. **Understand the Time Signature:**

* 4/2 time signature means there are 4 beats per measure.
* A half note receives one beat.

2. **Determine the Beat Pattern:**

* Beat 1: Strong
* Beat 2: Weak
* Beat 3: Medium
* Beat 4: Weak

3. **Complete the Measure Systematically:**

* **Measure 1:**
    * Beat 1: F3 (dotted half note) - complete
    * Beat 2: F2 (half note) - complete
    * Beat 3: F/ (eighth note) - incomplete
    * Beat 4: (Blank)

    * *Action:* Add an eighth rest after the F/ to complete beat 3.  A half rest fills the remaining space of beat 4.

* **Measure 2:**
    * Beat 1: (3FFF (dotted half note triplet) - complete
    * Beat 2: F2 (half note) - complete
    * Beats 3 & 4: (Blank)

    * *Action:* A whole rest fills beats 3 and 4.

* **Measure 3 (6/4):**
    * Beat 1: F<F (dotted half note) - complete
    * Beat 2: F2 (half note) - complete
    * Beat 3: F3 (dotted half note) - complete
    * Beat 4: (Blank)

    * *Action:* A half rest fills beat 4.

* **Measure 4:**
    * Beat 1: F2 (half note) - complete
    * Beat 2: F (quarter note) - incomplete
    * Beat 3: F (quarter note) - incomplete
    * Beat 4: F (quarter note) - incomplete
    * Additional: F2 (half note) - encroaches on the next measure, creating an incomplete beat.

    * *Action:* Add a quarter rest after the three quarter notes (F F F) to complete beat 4. The final F2 is addressed in the next measure.

* **Measure 5 (9/8):**
    * Beat 1: F/FF/ (Three eighth notes tied as a dotted quarter) - complete
    * Beat 2: F2 (quarter note) - complete
    * Beat 3: (Blank)
    * Beat 4: F/ (eighth note) - incomplete
    * Beat 5 & 6: F2 (quarter note) - complete
    * Beat 7: F (eighth note) - incomplete
    * Beat 8 & 9: F/F/F/F/F (5 sixteenth notes = a dotted eighth note tied to a sixteenth note) - complete

    * *Action:* Add an eighth rest to complete beat 3. Add a sixteenth rest after the F/ to complete beat 4. Add a sixteenth rest after the F to complete beat 7.

* **Measure 6 (3/4):**
    * Beat 1: F2 (half note) - complete
    * Beat 2 & 3: (Blank)

    * *Action:* A whole rest fills beats 2 and 3.

* **Measure 7 (3/4):**
