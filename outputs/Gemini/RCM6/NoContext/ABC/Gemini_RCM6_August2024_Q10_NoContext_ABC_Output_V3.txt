- Key: F major
- Time signature: 3/4
- Chord: Subdominant triad (Bb-D-F)
- Interval: Diminished fifth
- Functional chord: IV (subdominant)
- Chord: Submediant triad (Ab-C-Eb spelled as C-Eb-G)
- Interval: Perfect fifth
- Rest: Quarter rest
- Period: Parallel period
- Meaning:  *Allegretto* indicates a moderately fast tempo, somewhat slower than allegro. *Vivace* adds to this, meaning "lively" or "brisk."  Therefore, *allegretto vivace* suggests a tempo that is moderately fast and played with a lively character.
